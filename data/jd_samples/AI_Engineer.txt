1.AI Engineer
Role Overview
An AI Engineer designs, develops, deploys, and maintains artificial intelligence systems that solve real-world problems using machine learning, deep learning, and data-driven approaches.
Key Responsibilities
• Design and build AI/ML models for production use
• Collect, preprocess, and analyze structured and unstructured data
• Train, evaluate, and optimize machine learning models
• Deploy AI models using APIs, microservices, or cloud platforms
• Monitor model performance and retrain as needed
• Collaborate with product, data, and engineering teams
• Ensure scalability, security, and reliability of AI systems
Required Skills & Qualifications
• Strong knowledge of Python, ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn)
• Experience with data processing and feature engineering
• Understanding of ML algorithms and evaluation metrics
• Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
• Knowledge of REST APIs and system integration
Preferred Skills
• Experience with MLOps tools
• Knowledge of Docker, Kubernetes
• Exposure to NLP, Computer Vision, or GenAI
